## Clearable

Use the `isClearable` prop to add a cross button to remove the content (value) of the `TextInput`

<Playground>
  <Form initialValues={{ firstName: 'Trevor' }}>
    <ConnectedField
      component={InputText}
      isClearable
      label="First name"
      name="firstName"
      placeholder="Boaty"
    />
  </Form>
</Playground>
